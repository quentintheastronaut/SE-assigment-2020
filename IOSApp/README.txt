IOS App for user to pre-order their dish before arriving the court

<Login method> 
1. Register a account with email and login
2. Login by Google account 

<Main Screen>
- Slide the screen to view the menu.
- "Add to cart" button: to open the order view so that user can choose their option and then add it to the cart.
- "Cart" button: to open the cart view so user can check the thier order and change the quantity if they want.
- "Pay your order" button: to finish the order and open the payment view (coming soon)
- "Log out" button: to log out
- "Account" button: to open account manager view ( coming soon)
